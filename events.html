Tournament
📅 Oct 12 - 15, 2026 📍 Rohaniya Ground, Varanasi

S.N. Pandey Memorial Handball Cup

Annual flagship regional tournament open to registered school-level sub-junior and junior teams. Includes championship shield trophy, standard sports kits distributions, and merit plaques.

Tournament Notice: Entry slots close strictly on Sept 30. No spot entry fees. Medical certificates are mandatory for all athletes.
Fitness Camp
📅 June 15 - 20, 2026 📍 SNP Academy Campus

Summer Agility & Skill Handball Camp

An intensive 5-day skill development workshop led by Head Coach Vinod Kumar. Focuses on quick dribbling play, court offensive formations, high-vertical jumps, and muscle recovery guidance.

Age Requirement: Open for trainees aged 10-17 years (Boys & Girls). Fully funded study libraries books and dietary protein shakes are provided daily.
CSR Drive
📅 June 28, 2026 📍 Harhua Block Schools

Panchayat Drinking Water CSR Campaign

Collaborating with corporate sponsors to install 4 certified pure drinking water filtration zones inside primary panchayat schools, benefiting 1,000+ local children.

Employee Engagement: Corporate volunteers are welcome to assist the trustees in school site assemblies and clean water awareness distributions.
Trials
📅 July 15, 2026 📍 Varanasi Sports Complex

District Handball Selection Trials

Official selection trials to shortlist the best boys and girls handball talent to represent Varanasi District in the upcoming UP State sub-junior championships.

Trial Notices: Carry 2 passport photos, Birth Certificate xerox, and School ID cards. Selection trials begin at 08:00 AM sharp.
Health Camp
📅 August 05, 2026 📍 SNP Academy Care Cabin

Physiological Fitness & Health Screening

Quarterly physical checkup camps offering stamina endurance tests, vertical jump assessments, physical therapy recover clinics, and customized diet schedule charts.

Medical Crew: Led by Dr. Sneha Patel and volunteer physical physiotherapists. Free vitamin supplements distributed to all academy trainees.
CSR Drive
📅 August 20, 2026 📍 Chandpur School Campus

Varanasi Green Sports Tree Plantation

A cooperative drive to plant 500 shade-giving neem and fruit trees along the outer borders of government school handball grounds, creating clean, green sports environment.

Community Welfare: Supported by Mr. Rahul Yadav and educational volunteers. Saplings and planting tools are sponsored by trust donors.
