Founder & Chairman
Trust Visionary

Dr. A.K. Pandey

25+ Years of Collective Sports Advocacy
"Every child in our villages has the potential to represent India on global sports fields. We are simply the bridge providing them with the opportunity."

Established as a prestigious national level NGO & Sports Trust, S.N. Pandey Khel Sansthan Trust has focused on grooming elite handball talent under Dr. A.K. Pandey's mentorship. By ensuring rigorous training paths, modern kits, and absolute academic support, we cultivate athletic discipline alongside strong educational metrics.
