Welcome to Our Trust

Nurturing Athletics, Education & Grassroots Integration

S.N. Pandey Khel Sansthan Trust (Reg. No. 202400996036397) was established to transform the lives of youth in rural and underprivileged sectors. By combining systematic, professional handball training structures with high-quality educational support, we guide our athletes toward becoming elite national champions and highly responsible, productive citizens.

Making a Difference

Our Key Initiatives

Empowering rural youth and constructing strong community frameworks across diverse sectors.

Sports Development

Providing professional Olympic coaching pathways, athletic kits, and modern sports facilities at the grassroots level.

Handball Academy

Our flagship handball academy identifying elite young athletes and grooming them under Olympic standards.

Rural Education

Bridging academic gaps by supporting primary schools, supplying books, and organizing tuition setups.

Women Empowerment

Creating specialized sports clinics and leadership programs for girls to foster confidence and independence.

Health Awareness

Organizing local health camps, medical screenings, and distribution of sanitary supplies and nutrition guides.

Youth Skill Programs

Providing vocational training, digital literacy, and professional coaching certifications to secure livelihoods.

Environmental Campaigns

Hosting mass tree plantation drives, plastic-free sports matches, and community cleanliness campaigns.

Scholarship Programs

Sponsoring talented student athletes with academic fee waivers and complete high-performance training kits.

Leadership Message

Message From Our Founder & Chairman

"We believe that sports is not just a game, but a powerful catalyst that cultivates character, discipline, and community resilience."

Welcome to S.N. Pandey Khel Sansthan Trust. Our foundational objective is to discover the massive athletic talent locked inside our country's rural sectors, and give them the coaching, facilities, and academic support they deserve to represent India.

Through our collaborative efforts with corporate CSR partners, government bodies, and social workers, we are expanding our reach to build sustainable ecosystems where education and athletic excellence thrive hand-in-hand. We invite you to join us in guiding these future champions.

Dr. A.K. Pandey Founder & Chairman, S.N. Pandey Khel Sansthan Trust
