Where Your Money Goes

Donation Purpose & Impact

Every rupee contributed translates directly into measurable benefits for rural handball trainees in Varanasi blocks.

Handball Court Build

Funds standard clay court installations inside government village schools. Provides kids with clean, standard-dimension courts.

Adopt an Athlete

Sponsors a child's academy coaching fee, complete monthly physical training, daily high-protein diets, and protective sportswear.

Sports & Study Library

Finances textbooks, silent desks, and free book packages inside rural primary centers to ensure trainees maintain positive school grades.

Girls Sports Kits

Provides girls-only training clinics with sanitary hygiene packs, jerseys, court shoes, and protective knee supports.

Real Outcomes

Trainee Success Stories

Read how community donations directly changed the life path of our talented village trainees.

From Village Clay to State Shield

Rahul Yadav's Athletic Leap

Son of a marginal farmer in Rohaniya, Rahul Yadav trained on our clay court under Vinod Kumar. Lacking professional shoes or diet support, trust donors sponsored his complete daily diet, kit bags, and court gear.

Through physical conditioning, Rahul successfully qualified to lead the UP sub-junior handball team, winning theUP State Shield shield in 2025.

Girls Empowerment Story

Kiran Upadhyay's Goal

Residing in a backward sector, Kiran Upadhyay was exceptionally quick but lacked boarding support or school textbook packs. The trust sponsored her high-school academic libraries fees and court equipment.

Today, Kiran represents our female team lead and has successfully coached junior girls selection clinics, inspiring 50+ village girls to play handball.
