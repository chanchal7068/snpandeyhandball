About Our Trust

Nurturing Athletic Excellence & CSR Community Development

S.N. Pandey Khel Sansthan Trust

"Empowering youth and rural communities through the medium of sports, modern training standards, and holistic educational initiatives."

Established as a prestigious national level NGO & Sports Trust, S.N. Pandey Khel Sansthan Trust (Reg. No.: 202400996036397) has been at the forefront of identifying, grooming, and training elite youth handball athletes.

Our operations are focused heavily on bridging the gap between grassroots athletic potential and Olympic-level opportunities, while driving deep corporate social responsibility (CSR) initiatives in rural and underprivileged education, women empowerment, health awareness, and skills training.

Our History

Founded with a robust mission to create sustainable pathways for athletic and social success, the trust was born from the ideals of S.N. Pandey. Beginning with local grassroots handball leagues, the trust has grown to support and empower thousands of student athletes with systematic training, academic scholarships, and high-performance equipment.

Our Vision

To build national and global handball champions while cultivating highly responsible, disciplined, and educated citizens who drive progress in their respective communities. We visualize a future where sports is a primary catalyst for rural and social development, health, and national integration.

Our Mission

To construct world-class grassroots academies, run systematic sports and education programs, raise health and fitness standards in rural sectors, promote female athletic participation, and collaborate with CSR companies to fund high-impact social welfare projects.

Our Core Values

The foundations of our athletic and humanitarian efforts

Integrity & Transparency

Maintaining absolute honesty and accountability in all trust activities, sports selections, and financial allocations.

Sportsmanship & Grit

Fostering discipline, dedication, high-level sports ethics, and the resilience to face athletic and life challenges.

Inclusive Community Focus

Ensuring that gender, economic background, or geography never acts as a barrier to athletic training and education.
