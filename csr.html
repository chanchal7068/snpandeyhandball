CSR & Corporate Partnership

Aligning corporate vision with grassroots athletic development and village community welfare.

Schedule VII Compliance

CSR Collaboration Model

Under the provisions of Section 135 and Schedule VII of the Indian Companies Act, 2013, promotion of nationally recognized sports and rural development are fully approved CSR avenues. Our trust offers fully compliant, transparent corporate partnership routes designed to achieve measurable social goals.

By collaborating with S.N. Pandey Khel Sansthan Trust, companies can direct their CSR investments toward building sports infrastructure, establishing coaching academy facilities, and empowering women sports in Varanasi's rural blocks. We deliver 80G & 12A tax deductions and provide detailed impact audits for corporate board review.
